Post-Processing (PPH)

Rule-based heuristic processes probabilities in descending order to enforce topological consistency. Removes impossible lane-slot connections.

6 Rules Greedy Consistency

Post-Processing Heuristic Rules

1 No exit lanes to the left of an approach lane
2 No approach lanes to the right of an exit lane
3 No through or right turns to the left of a left turn
4 No left turns to the right of a through or right turn
5 No left or through turns to the right of a right turn
6 No duplicate turns from same approach to same exit slot

Loss Function Design

Weighted Cross-Entropy

Class weights derived from N/P ratios counteract severe imbalance. Weights capped at 30 to prevent instability.

wCE = min(N/P ratio, 30)

Soft Dice Loss

Complementary overlap-based loss that handles imbalanced classes without explicit weighting.

ℒ = ℒCE + ℒSD

"Almost Impossible" Masking

Adjacency cells with N/P ≥ 1000 receive reduced CE weight to prevent the model from focusing on near-impossible connections.

CE Interest: 0.40 CE Impossible: 0.20 Soft Dice: 0.40
